# Brilliance of Virat Kohli
- Today in the test match between India and South Africa, India won the toss and elected to bat first. There was a lot of outside noise around Virat Kohli's next century(his 71st)
- When he came out to bat, he not only did not show any signs of pressure but he came with the 'attack mode' on. He got out couple of times playing cover drives before in the series. 
This time, he was prepared. He had changed his foot placement whilst playing off-side shots. 
When he hit his first boundary(after 15 balls) playing his signature cover drive, his brilliance was on display. Right after playing the shot, he looked down to his feet to check if he had the proper foot placement. 
- It is important to note this because, he could have easily just enjoyed his glorious shot and carry on. He instead chose to make sure that his 'process' and his basic techniques were upto the mark or not.
- What I learned from his 79 run knock today is that, with patience, discipline, and intense focus, everything can be conquered. It would be normal to assume that he would play cautious cricket, hitting only the bad balls for boundaries. What makes Virat great is that he did exactly that, only with aggression. 
Imagine that, playing defensively with aggression and positivity. Now that's one of the many qualities that I adore (and try to replicate in my own life) in him.
